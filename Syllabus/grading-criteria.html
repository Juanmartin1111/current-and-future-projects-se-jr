GRADING POLICIES

No late assignments will be accepted. It is vastly preferable to submit a partially complete assignment on time than to turn in a late assignment.

Similarly, it is vastly more beneficially to turn in a programming or lab assignment that has 70% of the features fully implemented than to turn in an assignment that has 100% of the features partially implemented.

Course Policies

Class attendance is required unless otherwise specified. During our class time we will remain focused on the topics at hand, avoid utilizing phones or email, and be inclusive of our fellow classmates.

Changes to Course Assignments or Grades

Changes to the course assignments or grades will be communicated through course lecture and/or Blackboard announcements.
